RRR

RRR is a 2022 Indian Telugu-language action drama film directed by S. S. Rajamouli. It stars N. T. Rama Rao Jr. and Ram Charan as freedom fighters in a fictional story set in 1920s India. The film combines intense action, emotional depth, and powerful friendship. It showcases themes of patriotism, sacrifice, and resistance against British rule. RRR received global acclaim for its storytelling, visuals, and music.

SCAM 1992

Scam 1992 is a popular Indian web series based on the 1992 stock market scam. It tells the story of Harshad Mehta, a stockbroker who manipulated the market. Directed by Hansal Mehta, its adapted from the book "The Scam" by Debashis Basu and Sucheta Dalal. The series highlights greed, ambition, and flaws in the financial system. Praised for its storytelling and performances, it became a major hit.

LUCKY BASKHA

Lucky Baskhar is a 2024 Telugu crime drama film starring Dulquer Salmaan. It tells the story of a humble bank cashier who rises to wealth through financial scams. Set between 1989 and 1992, the film explores ambition, corruption, and personal transformation. Directed by Venky Atluri, it features intense storytelling and strong performances.
